Improve the receipt scanning logic for Indonesian receipts.

The current system sometimes detects incorrect numbers such as transaction IDs instead of the real payment total.

Implement a robust receipt parsing system.

---

STEP 1 — PREPROCESS OCR TEXT

Normalize OCR output:

* convert text to lowercase
* remove "rp", "idr"
* replace commas and dots properly
* remove duplicate spaces

---

STEP 2 — DETECT TOTAL USING KEYWORDS

Search lines that contain these keywords:

Primary keywords:

total
total bayar
grand total
jumlah
total pembayaran
total belanja
total rp

Secondary keywords:

payment
amount
amount due
total due

If a line contains these keywords:

extract the number from the same line.

---

STEP 3 — SUBTOTAL FALLBACK

If no total found:

search lines with:

subtotal
sub total
jumlah sementara

Use this value as fallback.

---

STEP 4 — NUMBER EXTRACTION

Extract numbers with regex:

\d{1,3}([.,]\d{3})+

Convert:

43,500
43.500

into integer:

43500

---

STEP 5 — INVALID NUMBER FILTER

Ignore numbers that are likely:

transaction ID
invoice number
phone number

Apply filters:

ignore numbers > 1,000,000

ignore numbers with more than 7 digits

---

STEP 6 — FINAL FALLBACK

If still not found:

extract all numbers from OCR text.

remove numbers:

> 1,000,000

select the largest remaining number.

---

STEP 7 — DATE DETECTION

Detect date formats:

dd/mm/yyyy
dd-mm-yyyy
dd month yyyy

Example:

05/03/2026
10 May 2019

---

STEP 8 — MERCHANT DETECTION

Merchant is usually located in the first 3 lines of the receipt.

Take the first line that:

* contains letters
* is not numeric
* length > 3 characters

---

STEP 9 — OUTPUT STRUCTURE

Return structured data:

merchant
total
date
items (optional)

---

IMPORTANT

The parser must prioritize numbers appearing on the same line as TOTAL keywords.

Never treat transaction IDs or order numbers as payment totals.

The goal is to correctly detect totals from Indonesian receipts such as:

Indomaret
Alfamart
BreadTalk
Starbucks
KFC
McDonald's
Superindo
Transmart

---

This system must work reliably with Indonesian retail receipts.
